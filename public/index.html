StandCore: The future of real-time developer stand-ups

Traditional stand-ups are slow, repetitive, and disruptive especially for fast-moving, distributed teams. StandCore replaces daily meetings with asynchronous check-ins that are quick to log and powerful to track.

With smart summaries, blocker detection, and seamless integration with GitHub, Jira, and other tools, StandCore ensures every update is captured, every blocker surfaced, and no detail lost in the noise.

Built for developers, not managers, StandCore gives your team the freedom to focus, communicate clearly, and stay aligned, no calls, no pings, just progress.
